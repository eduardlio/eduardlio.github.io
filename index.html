Current Project: Real Engine

A game engine for the real world

In a partnership with the University of Queensland and Real Engine, we have begun developing a visual scripting language for Real Engine: The Game engine for the real world. By using actors, RFID chips and scanners and GPS, users are completely and literally immersed in the game.

My role as designer of the team is to design the overall "language" of the system. The challenge of this project lies in its interpretability but I'm determined that this project will give me a lot of room to explore the different aspects of interaction design.

To stay in the loop with this project, feel free to contact me at liao.edward@gmail.com
